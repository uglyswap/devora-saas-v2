# Funnel Optimization Strategy - Devora

**Framework:** AARRR (Pirate Metrics)
**Last Updated:** December 9, 2025

---

## AARRR Funnel Overview

```
Acquisition → Activation → Revenue → Retention → Referral
```

---

## 2025 Targets

| Stage | Metric | Current | Target |
|-------|--------|---------|--------|
| **Acquisition** | Monthly signups | 500 | 10,000 |
| **Activation** | First generation within 7 days | 60% | 75% |
| **Revenue** | Free-to-paid conversion | 3% | 5% |
| **Retention** | Monthly churn (Pro) | 8% | <5% |
| **Referral** | Users who refer | 5% | 20% |

---

## Stage 1: Acquisition

**Channels:**
1. **SEO** (40% of traffic target)
2. **Content Marketing** (25%)
3. **Paid Ads** (20%)
4. **Partnerships** (15%)

**Key Actions:**
- Publish 20 blog posts/month
- Target 50 high-intent keywords
- Launch Google Ads campaign

---

## Stage 2: Activation

**"Aha Moment":** Generate first component in <5 minutes

**Optimizations:**
- One-click demo (no signup required)
- Guided first generation
- Quick-start templates

---

## Stage 3: Revenue

**Conversion Levers:**
- Usage limit nudges (9/10 generations)
- Feature gating (VS Code Extension)
- Annual discount (save 16%)

---

## Stage 4: Retention

**Actions:**
- Weekly usage reports
- New feature announcements
- Churn prevention emails

---

## Stage 5: Referral

**Referral Program:**
- Referrer: 1 month free Pro
- Referee: Extended 30-day trial
