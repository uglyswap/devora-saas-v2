## Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Devora SAS ("we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website https://devora.io and use our Service.

By using our Service, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this Policy.

---

## Types of Cookies We Use

### 1. Essential Cookies (Required)
| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `devora_session` | Keeps you logged in | 30 days |
| `csrf_token` | Protects against CSRF attacks | Session |
| `cookie_consent` | Remembers your cookie preferences | 1 year |

### 2. Analytics Cookies (Optional - Requires Consent)
| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `ph_*` | PostHog analytics (self-hosted) | 1 year |
| `_ga` | Google Analytics (if enabled) | 2 years |

### 3. Marketing Cookies (Optional - Requires Consent)
| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `_gcl_*` | Google Ads conversion tracking | 90 days |
| `_fbp` | Facebook Pixel (retargeting) | 90 days |

## Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner with options to:
- Accept All
- Reject Optional
- Customize

**You can change your preferences anytime:** Settings > Privacy > Cookie Preferences
