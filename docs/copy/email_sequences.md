# Email Sequences - Devora

**Lifecycle Email Marketing**
**Last Updated:** December 9, 2025

---

## Email Strategy Overview

### Sequences
1. **Onboarding** (5 emails over 14 days)
2. **Activation** (3 emails for inactive users)
3. **Upgrade** (4 emails for free users hitting limits)
4. **Churn Prevention** (3 emails for at-risk users)
5. **Re-engagement** (3 emails for dormant users)
6. **Win-back** (2 emails for cancelled users)
